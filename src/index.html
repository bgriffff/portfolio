MHTech and TechWiki

I revamped and redesigned an existing software product called the TechWiki (for how-to articles). The goal was to make it faster and easier for product managers to create how-to articles for customers. Instead of taking months to get how-to articles approved to be live, with the new and improved TechWiki, it took minutes. I also redesigned the MHTech website (for overview articles) to create a better user experience. It is now a seamless experience to go from overview articles (MHTech website) to how-to articles (TechWiki).
